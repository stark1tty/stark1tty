### About

I recently passed my PhD viva at the University of Plymouth and am currently looking for new opportunities. My thesis, *Deconstructing the Wildscape*, examines the cultural heritage and Holocene palaeoecology of the Humberhead Levels ‚Äî challenging the divide between "pristine" and human-altered landscapes. My research sits at the intersection of geoarchaeology, palaeoecology, and geomatics, and I'm also interested in open-source tools, machine learning for environmental science, and science communication.
